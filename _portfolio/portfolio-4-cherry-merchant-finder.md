Won the **2025 Cherry New Grad Hackathon**.

- Developed an interactive map-based interface to help consumers find merchants who offer Cherry's services.
- The solution featured a map view with a sidebar for detailed merchant information, as well as filtering by distance and services.
- Led a team of new graduates to design and implement a fully functional demo in under 3 hours.
